Still.css is water.css but better. I use this as a base and add custom styles as I go.

Installation

Paste this into the <head> of your HTML: 

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://github.com/kognise/water.css">

  • Responsive

  • Good browser support

Is it responsive?

Heck yeah! It doesn't include any fancy styles so it's easily mobile responsive. Just add the famous 

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
and you'll be good to go!
